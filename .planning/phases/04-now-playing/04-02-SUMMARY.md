## Health-Check Semantics (D-12, chosen + why)

**Chosen: Pattern 3 option (a) — "a callback arrived at all" = healthy.**

The Swift wrapper (`ejbills/mediaremote-adapter`) does NOT surface ungive's `test` subcommand, so a direct "is the bridge alive?" probe is unavailable. Two facts force a synthesized signal:
1. **Pitfall 1:** `getTrackInfo`'s `nil` is ambiguous — it means *either* "no media playing" (healthy) *or* "the bridge failed" (unavailable). The value alone can't distinguish them.
2. **Pitfall 2:** `onListenerTerminated` fires only after `eventCount != 0`, so a child that is blocked-at-launch and never emits will never trigger it.

Therefore `runHealthCheck` races a one-shot `getTrackInfo` against a 3s `asyncAfter` timeout, guarded by a `settled` flag: the first to fire wins. A callback (even with `nil` info) within 3s → `setHealthy(true)`; the timeout firing first → `setHealthy(false)` (D-12 "nicht verfügbar"). This is a documented design choice (A3), not a guess — mid-session death is handled separately by `onListenerTerminated` (D-13).

## Adapter-API Notes (for Plan 04)

- **All callbacks are already main-dispatched by the wrapper** (confirmed: every `onTrackInfoReceived` / `getTrackInfo` invocation is inside a `DispatchQueue.main.async` in `MediaController.swift`). Plan 04 must NOT add another main-hop around the closures, but can safely touch `@Published`/AppKit inside them.
- `TrackInfo.Payload.artwork` is a **pre-decoded `NSImage?`** (the wrapper does `NSImage(data:)` from the base64 field). Our code never touches raw artwork bytes — invalid bytes yield `artwork == nil` → `hasArtwork: false` → Plan 03 placeholder (T-04-06).
- `startListening()` internally auto-restarts the child on certain stream errors (`restartListeningProcess`), but `onListenerTerminated` is the explicit mid-death signal we rely on for D-13. Plan 04 should treat `onTerminated` as authoritative for flipping `isHealthy = false`.
- The wrapper exposes far more transport (`play/pause/stop/setTime/seek/like/ban/shuffle/repeat`); only `togglePlayPause/nextTrack/previousTrack` are wired (NOW-02). Star & seek are deliberately dropped/v2 (CONTEXT.md) — do not surface them in Plan 03/04 without a scope change.
