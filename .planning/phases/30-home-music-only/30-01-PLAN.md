<objective>
Give the Home view its own last-played and empty sub-states: add the sticky `lastKnownTrack`
data contract, add `.homeLastPlayed`/`.homeEmpty` cases to the single-arbiter `resolve()`
reducer (replacing `.expandedIdle`'s Home-fallback role), and wire `NotchPillView`'s body
switch to render them ŌĆö the last-played state through the SAME `mediaExpanded(_:art:)` view
the live state already uses (D-04), the empty state through a new `homeEmptyState` copied from
`trayEmptyState`'s exact template (D-09/D-10, LOCKED copy).

Purpose: HOME-02 and HOME-03 both live entirely in this one reducer branch + its two render
arms ŌĆö per this project's single-arbiter discipline (Phase 6 D-05, reused by every subsequent
phase: 17, 18, 26, 28), the last-played/empty decision must be made in `resolve()`, never as an
`if` inside the view.
Output: `resolve()` correctly classifies all 3 Home sub-states (playing/paused unchanged,
last-played, empty); `NotchPillView` renders the two new states with real, final content (not
placeholders); the old idle time/weather/calendar glance and its exclusively-used helpers are
deleted; `IslandResolverTests.swift` covers the new branch.
</objective>
