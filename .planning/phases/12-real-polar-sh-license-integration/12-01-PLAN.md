<objective>
Create the Keychain-backed persistence layer for the validated license: a `LicenseStore`
protocol seam, a `LicenseRecord` Codable value, a real `KeychainLicenseStore` (mirroring the
proven `KeychainTrialStore`), and a `LicenseManager` singleton that reads the persisted record
ONCE and caches it in memory. This is the "offline-after-first-validation" (LIC-02 / success
criterion 3) foundation and the T-11-02 mitigation (entitlement is a Codable record in the
Keychain, never a flippable bool, never UserDefaults).

Purpose: subsequent plans persist a granted record here on successful validation (12-03) and
short-circuit `LicenseState` to `.licensed` from this cache at launch with zero network I/O.
Output: `Islet/Licensing/KeychainLicenseStore.swift`, `IsletTests/LicenseManagerTests.swift`.
</objective>
