|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-34-01 | Tampering | `quickActionPreview` filename/count display | mitigate | Reuse `ShelfItemView`'s exact V5 convention (`.lineLimit(1)`, `.truncationMode(.middle)`, `.frame(maxWidth:)`) — same mitigation already proven for the shelf row, applied verbatim here (Task 3) |
