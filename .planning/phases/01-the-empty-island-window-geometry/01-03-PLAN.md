<open_questions_to_resolve>
From RESEARCH.md Assumptions Log + Open Questions:
- **A2 (Medium risk):** `.statusBar` may or may not render the pill OVER the Tahoe (macOS 26)
  transparent/floating menu bar in the notch band. Prior-art differs: DynamicNotchKit uses
  `.screenSaver`, boring.notch uses `.mainMenu + 3`, CLAUDE.md hint is `.statusBar`. If the pill
  is clipped/occluded exactly at the notch, the fix is a ONE-LINE change: `level = .mainMenu + 1`.
- **A3 (Medium risk):** Hide-in-clamshell relies on the built-in display dropping out of
  `NSScreen.screens` when the lid closes (external monitor attached). If it lingered, the panel
  could try to render on an off display. Needs a real plug-external-then-close-lid test.
</open_questions_to_resolve>
</context>
