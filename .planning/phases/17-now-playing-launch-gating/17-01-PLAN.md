<objective>
Implement NOW-04: Islet must not auto-show the ambient Now Playing "wings" glance for a track
that has only ever been paused/loaded since launch. The glance appears only once a `.playing`
presentation has actually been observed at least once during the current Islet process
lifetime. A manual expand always reveals the real state regardless of the gate (D-03), and once
lifted the gate never re-arms for the rest of the session (D-02).

Purpose: Fixes the false-positive glance shown today for players that restore a paused session
on Islet launch — the ambient glance must only ever announce a genuine Play, not a stale
loaded-but-paused track.
Output: `hasPlayedSinceLaunch` flag on `NowPlayingState`, a new `nowPlayingLaunchGate(...)` pure
helper + updated `resolve(...)` signature in `IslandResolver.swift`, the controller wiring that
flips and threads the flag, full `IslandResolverTests.swift` regression coverage, and an
on-device verification of all 3 ROADMAP Phase 17 success criteria.
</objective>
