<objective>
Wire the FOCUS-SAFE interaction (ISL-03): make the pill expand on CLICK with the spring morph,
fire a trackpad haptic + bounce on hover-enter WITHOUT expanding (D-01/D-02), collapse after a
grace delay on pointer-leave (D-03), and — non-negotiably — never activate Islet or steal focus,
keeping clicks OUTSIDE the pill passing through (D-04).

geometry it uses (`expandedNotchFrame`) were proven in Plan 02-01; the morph view it animates was
built in Plan 02-02. This plan is the AppKit glue connecting them.

        guard let target = selectTargetScreen(from: descriptors),
              let frame = notchFrame(screenFrame: target.frame, safeAreaTop: target.safeAreaTop,
