<objective>
Add the pure, additive secondary-activity output to the resolver layer (D-01/D-02/D-03/D-04/D-10)
and its `@Published` carrier. This is the phase's Wave 0/contract plan: every other plan in this
phase (view rendering in 42-03, controller wiring in 42-04) consumes `SecondaryActivity` and
`resolveSecondary(...)` as a fixed interface, so this plan ships first and in isolation.

Purpose: Establish the single, pure "which activity is secondary right now" decision as a TOTAL,
Foundation-only function that structurally cannot disagree with `resolve()`'s own primary verdict
— per PITFALLS.md's "one pure arbiter" discipline and 42-RESEARCH.md's Pitfall 1 warning about two
independently-mutated `@Published` fields drifting out of sync.
Output: `SecondaryActivity` enum, `resolveSecondary(primary:nowPlaying:)`, `IslandPresentationState.secondary`, 5 new passing XCTest methods.
</objective>
